Governance at BNB Learning Center

A structured system for excellence and accountability.

A Structured System for Excellence and Accountability

BNB Learning Center operates under a well-defined governance structure that ensures transparency, accountability, and strategic direction. Governance at BNB is designed to create a stable and effective educational institution, balancing strong leadership with community involvement.

The Role of the Board of Directors

The Board of Directors serves as the highest decision-making body at BNB. Comprising five experienced members, the board is responsible for:

  • Setting the strategic vision and approving key policies.
  • Overseeing financial management to ensure sustainability.
  • Appointing and evaluating the Chief Executive Officer (CEO).
  • Ensuring compliance with national and international education standards.

Governance Structure & Committees

BNB follows a hierarchical governance model, ensuring smooth operations and decision-making.

  • Chief Executive Officer (CEO) – Leads the execution of the strategic plan and reports to the board.
  • Executive Management Committee – Comprises senior managers ensuring daily operations align with strategic goals.
  • Academic Services Committee – Oversees curriculum, teacher development, and student performance.
  • Financial Management & Administration – Focuses on budgeting, risk assessment, and financial transparency.

Policies and Decision-Making

BNB governance is guided by structured policies that ensure:

  • Student and staff protection – Policies safeguard student welfare and maintain a positive work environment.
  • Fair resource allocation – Funds are managed responsibly to support school growth and quality education.
  • Ethical leadership – Decision-making is based on integrity, accountability, and inclusivity.
