Leadership at BNB Learning Center

Empowering future leaders through excellence in governance and innovation.

A Visionary Leadership for Educational Excellence

BNB Learning Center’s leadership is built on a foundation of integrity, innovation, and excellence. The institution prioritizes effective governance and strong management, ensuring that every decision aligns with its mission of empowering future leaders through world-class education.

The BNB Board of Directors

The Board of Directors oversees the institution’s strategic direction and governance. This five-member board plays a crucial role in:

  • Approving key policies, budgets, and long-term strategies.
  • Evaluating the performance of senior leadership.
  • Ensuring BNB remains financially sustainable and academically competitive.

Executive Leadership Team

The Executive Management Committee, led by the CEO, ensures effective implementation of BNB’s mission and vision. The team includes:

  • Chief Executive Officer (CEO) – Leads growth, community relations, and financial performance.
  • Education Services Director – Maintains academic excellence and supervises principals.
  • Finance & Administration Director – Manages budgeting, reporting, and financial sustainability.
  • Training & Consultancy Director – Expands BNB’s impact through training and consultancy programs.
  • Learning Advancement & Technology Manager – Oversees ICT, digital learning, and innovation.

School-Level Leadership

Each school division (Kindergarten, Elementary, Middle, and High School) has its own Principal and Deputy Principal, focusing on:

  • Maintaining academic excellence & discipline.
  • Encouraging student engagement & extracurricular activities.
  • Supporting teacher mentorship & professional development.

The Role of Parents and the Community

Leadership extends beyond administrators. Parents, community leaders, and businesses contribute through mentorship programs, scholarships, and partnerships.
